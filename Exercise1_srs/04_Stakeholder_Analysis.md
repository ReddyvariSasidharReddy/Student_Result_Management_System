# Stakeholder Analysis

# Primary Stakeholders

Students – Access results, performance graphs, and academic guidance.

Faculty Members – Enter marks, analyze student performance, and provide guidance.

Academic Administrators – Manage records, monitor overall performance, and generate reports.

# Secondary Stakeholders

Institution Management – Use reports for academic planning and decision-making.

IT Support Team – Maintain the system, database, and security.

Parents/Guardians – Review student academic progress and performance insights.
